Game Character Test

Introduction

This website helps you to know which video game character you are. There are some questions below that you need to answer to reveal your game character's identity. There are more than 30 game characters that you may be one of them.

Psychology

Your game character will be selected for you based on your traits. There are some questions that are based on humanistic psychology. When you submit your answers, the website will analyze your answers and then compare them to the moral codes of the game characters. All these characters are original video game protagonists so there are no vilians, side game characters or comic super heroes in this website.

Questions
Answer these 10 questions carefully.

1. If someone kills one of your beloved ones, Do you like to take revenge or just forgive?

2. Are you a patient person?

3. Do you like to rush into things and perform your tasks quick as quickly as possible?

4. Are you talkative?

5.In shooter video games, Which one do you prefer?

6. Do you usually act like the funny guy or the serious guy?

7. Do you consider yourself as an introvert or an extrovert?

8. Are you bad-temper?

9. Do you take risks?

10. Do you plan ahead and analyze consequences before doing something or making decisions?
